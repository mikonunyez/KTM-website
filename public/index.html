KTM CARAGA BROTHERS

Founded in 2019, KTM Caraga Brothers is a group of motorcycle lovers based in Caraga. We love going on rides, talking about bikes, or just hanging out. The group has also organized donation drives for people in need during the pandemic.

To date, the group is composed of around 70 KTM-loving members
